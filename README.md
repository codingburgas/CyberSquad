<h1 align="center">CyberSquad</h1>
 
<br>
<p>Hello, we are CyberSquad!</p>
<p>CyberSquad is a team of young, ambitious programmers dedicated to building smart, practical software solutions. Driven by curiosity and teamwork, we developed a desktop application that automatically organizes and sorts student grades, making academic management faster, clearer, and more efficient. Our goal is to combine clean design, reliable performance, and real‑world usefulness — proving that innovation doesn’t need to be complicated to make an impact.

Beyond coding, CyberSquad thrives on collaboration, problem‑solving, and constant learning. Each member brings a unique skill set, and together we turn ideas into functional tools that solve real challenges in education. We’re committed to improving our craft, exploring new technologies, and pushing ourselves to create software that genuinely helps students and teachers.</p>
